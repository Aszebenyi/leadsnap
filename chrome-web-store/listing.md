## Full description

**Win more local jobs by being the first to reply.**

LeadSnap monitors your Facebook groups 24/7 and texts you the moment someone posts looking for a service you offer — with an AI-written reply ready to copy and send.

---

### How it works

1. **Install LeadSnap** and sign in with Google or email
2. **Connect your Facebook groups** — LeadSnap detects the groups you're already in
3. **Add your keywords** — things like "lawn mowing", "dog boarding", "deep clean"
4. **Add your phone number** — LeadSnap texts you every time there's a match
5. **Reply first, win the job**

---

### What you get

✅ **Instant SMS alerts** — get a text the moment a matching post appears, with the post preview and group name included

✅ **AI-generated replies** — every alert includes a ready-to-use reply personalised to the post and your business. Just copy and paste.

✅ **Buyer intent score** — each lead is scored 1–10 so you know which ones are worth dropping everything for

✅ **Works with any local service** — plumbing, electrical, lawn care, cleaning, dog boarding, painting, moving, pest control, handyman, and more

✅ **Set it and forget it** — LeadSnap scans in the background every 10 minutes while Chrome is open. No manual checking required.

## Permission justifications

### storage
Saves your login session, selected Facebook groups, keywords, phone number, and settings locally so they persist between browser sessions.

### alarms
Schedules the background scan every 10 minutes and refreshes your login session every 55 minutes, even when the popup is closed.

### tabs
Opens your Facebook groups feed during onboarding so LeadSnap can detect which groups you're in, and opens the web dashboard when you click through from the popup.

### notifications
Shows a Chrome notification as a fallback after Google sign-in completes, in case the popup cannot be automatically reopened.

### windows
Creates a temporary background window to load Facebook group pages during a manual scan trigger.

### identity
Handles the Google OAuth sign-in flow securely via chrome.identity.launchWebAuthFlow, keeping your Google credentials out of the extension entirely.

### Host permission — facebook.com
The content script runs on Facebook to read post text from groups you're already a member of and match it against your keywords. LeadSnap never modifies, posts, or interacts with Facebook on your behalf.

### Host permission — supabase.co
Used for email/password sign-in and session management via the Supabase authentication API.

### Host permission — oauth2.googleapis.com
Used to complete the Google OAuth token exchange after sign-in.
